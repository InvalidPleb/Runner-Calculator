This calculator takes in two pieces of information and finds the third. For example, to calculate your pace, you would enter the distance you ran and the time it took you to run it, while leaving the pace field empty. You can calculate distance or time in the same way.

Press the Calculate button to get your result

What is pace?

    Running pace is the number of minutes that it takes to cover a mile or kilometer. Finding your pace is useful for completing a running event in a desired time frame. For example, if your pace is 10 minutes per mile, you'll know that your marathon time will be about 4.2 hours.

    Pace is also useful for training purposes. Running at different paces can offer different forms of running exercise, from walking pace to sprinting pace.
