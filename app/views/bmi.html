What is your BMI?

    Your BMI or Body Mass Index is a value intended to give you an idea of what a normal weight should be for your height, and whether you fall in that normal range or not. BMI is calculated by dividing your mass (weight) by your height squared.

Below 18.5 = Underweight

18.5 - 24.9 = Normal

25 - 29.9 = Overweight

30.0+ = Obese
